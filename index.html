About Us

Iowa City Robotics (FRC Team 167) is the oldest high school robotics team in the state of Iowa. Composed of STEM industry mentors and students from several schools in the Iowa City area, the team works to build a competitive robot in just over six weeks, promote STEM education throughout the community, and encourage STEM careers among team members.

Sponsors

Team 167 would not be able to function without the gracious support of sponsors from all over the community. The team's sponsors have promoted STEM education, fostered an appreciation for STEM, and made an investment in the workforce of the future.
